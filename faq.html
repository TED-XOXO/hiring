Do You Have Questions?

We have answers (well, most of the times!)

Below you'll find answers to the most common questions you may have on Hiring. Also please feel free to check our Social media pages. If you still can't find the answersb you are looking please contact us!

About Hiring

What are the benefits of Hiring?

Hiring is your one stop shop for planning and reaching your career related goals. The Hiring service is designed to help career builders to accelerate their growth through personalised learning, career guidance and accurate job recommendations.

Is Hiring a paid-for service?

Using Hiring is free of charge, outside of the data bundles needed to access the service. You can create your online CV, apply for jobs, read articles and access learning content on Hiring absolutely free.

What do I need to do to start using Hiring?

There are no special requirements for joining Hiring. Anyone above 18 years who wants to apply for jobs, learn new skills, stay up-to-date with industry news or who wants to plan ahead their career may register on Hiring. Registration is fast and easy. However, to find relevant jobs and courses or to increase chances of finding employment, we encourage you to complete your Hiring profile.

Can I use Hiring even if I am a student and don't have work experience?

Yes, you can. We have internship positions on the platform as well. You should also check the learning library that is designed to help you out in finding your first job opportunity. You can also read articles on your industry to prepare you for the career ahead.

Jobs on Hiring

What kind of jobs are there on Hiring

Hiring is open for jobs from all industries and seniority levels. We recommend that you check for new job content regularly. We have Hiring Exclusive jobs that have been posted by our employer partners as well as jobs we have aggregated from other sources. It is important to note that the aggregated jobs take you to an external website to post the application or provide you with instructions for sending in your application package. We make sure that the jobs on the platform are free from scams. Under no circumstances should you ever pay for applying for a job!

Am I allowed to apply for jobs in a country where I am not living at the moment?

Yes, you can. However, it is good to keep in mind that employers have the ultimate say on whether they are willing to deal with work permits and relocation costs. So make sure you check the requirements before submitting an application.

Are the jobs on Hiring different from other job sites?

Companies who post jobs on Hiring go through a validation process by the Fuzu team. They may also post the same jobs across multiple job sites. However, many employers use Hiring as their primary recruitment channel. Keep your Hiring profile updated as employers are searching the talent database and the stronger your profile, the better chances you have for landing your next job opportunity.

Password and account

How do I reset my password?

Open your browser and go to www.hiring.com/login. Click 'Forgot your password?' and follow the instructions to reset it. The system will send you a recovery email with a link to reset your password.

I am trying to log in but it says my email address is already taken?

Most likely you are on the registration page, please go to the login page and click on this link www.Hiring.com/login.Type in your username and password. If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by clicking on 'Forgot your password?'.

I don't need Hiring anymore. How do I delete my account?

We will be sad to see you leave and recommend that you keep your account, as we are constantly updating the content on Hiring to better serve our community's needs. I you nevertheless wish to delete your Hiring account, click on your image on the home page (header bar-top right) and select Account Settings. You will find the Delete account option from there.
